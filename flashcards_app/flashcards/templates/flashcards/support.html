FAQs
What is QuickCards?
QuickCards revision software is a digital tool designed to assist with memorization and learning through the use of virtual flashcards. Users can create digital flashcards with a question on one side and an answer on the other side.
How does it work?
The software allows users to review these flashcards at their own pace, testing their own knowledge and comprehension. It's a convenient and effective way to study and retain information.
Can I create and customize my own cards?
Yes, you can create and customize your own cards with QuickCards. Most software platforms offer features that allow you to input a question and an answer, add images or diagrams.
